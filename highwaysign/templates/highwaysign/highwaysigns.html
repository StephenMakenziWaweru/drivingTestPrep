Highway Signs

Highway signs or Traffic signs or road signs are signs erected at the side of or above roads to give instructions or provide information to road users.

They are classified into three major categories or classes

  • Class A: Also known as regulatory signs

    • As the name suggests - they give orders or direction into what should be done by road users

  • Class B: Also known as warning signs

    • Similarly - they give warning or inform about hazardsto road users

  • Class C: Also known as informatory signs

    • Information giving - they give information or inform road users about facilities available to them
